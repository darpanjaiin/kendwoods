🚗 How to Reach

  • By Car: Just 2 hours drive from Mumbai and 3.5 hours from Surat. The location is easily accessible via NH48 and offers a scenic drive through rural Maharashtra.
  • By Train: The nearest major railway station is Palghar Railway Station (approximately 40 km). From there, you can hire a taxi to reach the farmstay.
  • By Bus: Regular bus services are available from Mumbai to Vikramgad. From the bus stop, you can hire an auto-rickshaw or taxi to reach the property.
  • By Air: Nearest airport is at Mumbai. You can hire a cab from the airport to the farmstay.

Things To Do

AT KENWOOD'S FARMSTAY

GAMES ROOM

"Challenge your sibling or dad to Table Tennis or enjoy a relaxing game of Carrom, Cricket kit, Badminton rackets, Board Games, and Books are available for your leisure, catering to all interests."

CYCLE

"Discover the surroundings on two wheels – bicycles are readily available for your exploration and enjoyment during your stay."

TRACTOR RIDE

Enjoy a unique and exciting tractor ride, perfect for family fun. Hire the local tractor to journey to Sajan Dam, where you can bask in the splendor of an untouched, serene lake. An hour of pure beauty awaits.

SWIMMING POOL

"Take a refreshing dip in our swimming pool, perfect for cooling off on warm days or enjoying some leisurely laps. The pool area offers a relaxing space for both adults and children to enjoy aquatic fun."

FEEDING DUCKS/FISH

"For a leisurely moment, engage in feeding the koi fish and meet Donny and Daisy, our friendly ducks, in their tranquil pond setting."

NATURE WALK

"Explore the 200-meter rustic walking path around the property to reconnect with nature and maintain your fitness."

BEYOND KENWOOD'S

SAJAN DAM

"Just a brief 10-minute drive from Kenwoods lies the secluded Sajan Dam reservoir, an ideal haven for nature enthusiasts."

DHABOSA WATERFALLS

"Situated 14 kilometers away from Jawhar, this is one of the tallest perennial waterfalls near Mumbai. Plunging from a height of 300 feet, it holds a mesmerizing charm, especially in the monsoon season."

JAWAHAR FORT

"The Jai Vilas Palace, an enchanting historic tourist spot, crowns the hilltop. It's architecture gracefully blends Indian and Western styles. If you have a few days to spare at the farm, dedicating an hour's drive for this visit is truly rewarding."

WARLI ART

"Vikramgad serves as the birthplace and hub of the enduring tribal art, still actively pursued. Rooted in nature and agrarian living, this art style features red clay walls and white flour-water paint, applied using bamboo sticks."
